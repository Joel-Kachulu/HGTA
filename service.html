CROSS BOARDER ROAD FREIGHT

All cross border loads require a cross border permit when travelling by road. There are a number of requirements which a logistics company needs to adhere to if they wish to effectively transport goods across borders. If you were to transport loads exceeding 3500 kilograms, a cross border permit is required.

WAREHOUSING

Warehousing is an essential process when it comes to supply chain management and is critical to most logistics operations. As a market leading logistic compa- ny HGTA Logistics is in posession of warehouse facilities for the storage of clients goods on the premises.

CUSTOMS CLEARENCE

Whenever products or goods are imported or exported they need to pass through customs. Compiling the necessary documentation, paying customs duties and ensuring that the load has been paid for are vital components in the clearing process. This is where a logistics company such as HGTA Logistics can come in handy as we constantly deal with such issues on a daily basis

PACKAGING

Packaging is an important process which needs to be followed in any logistical op- eration. Effective packaging conserves space for larger deliveries while also protecting products and commodities during transportation. No matter whether goods are being shipped by sea, air or surface transport packaging is essential.
