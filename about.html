About Us

Why You Should Trust Us? Get Know About Us!

How Great Thou Art is a Malawian Registered transportation and Logistics Company that was started by Peter Ajusu in 2008. What started off as a local trucking Company eventually moved into a Cross Border Trucking business

HGTA currently transports goods within South Africa and across to Malawi, Zambia, Mozambique, Zimbabawe and Congo. Ensuring to deliver your goods safetly and in the same condition that the goods were recieved in.

Leading Transportation Company

Our aim to be a top Transport Company , transporting goods across the Southern African region and across Africa as a whole.
